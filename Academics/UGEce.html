ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

Electronics & Communication Department was established in the year 1999 with an intake of 120 students & M.Tech in VLSI Design and Embedded Systems and M.Tech in Electronics with an intake of 18 students. It is affiliated to Visveswaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi. ECE department is a vital and pulsating department with highly competent, experienced and dedicated faculty members. The department meets the growing requirement of practical design engineers in the country and abroad. The curriculum in Electronics and Communication Engineering, framed by VTU lays greater emphasis on design principles and development of communication models, signal processing, image processing, VLSI and embedded systems.

DYNAMIC FACULTY

The strength of the department lies in its staff. A majority of the faculty have Ph.D from elite universities and institutions from India and many come with a strong R&D background. The quality of teaching and training has yielded high growth rate of placement at various Companies. The major areas of faculty expertise of the department include Digital Electronics and Communication Systems, Optical Communication and Networks, Signal Processing, Image Processing, Power Electronics, Microwaves, VLSI and Embedded Systems etc.

INDUSTRY INTEGRATED LEARNING PROGRAM(IILP)

Industry Integrated Learning Program (IILP) is an initiative at East Point to bridge the gap between Academia & Industry. The department is associated with companies like CISCO CCNA, ARM University, etc. Dedicated faculties are assigned as educators from the companies are trained, certified and are authorized to train the students on the courses. IILP courses are offered to students right from the 2 nd year of Engineering, so that by the end of engineering, students are enriched with wide variety of technical skills. Centre of Excellence lab for CISCO, ARM University are set up within the campus.

VIBRANT LEARNING EXPERIENCES

ECE Department is actively involved with the student’s growth through regular Parents-Teacher interaction, Distinction Award Ceremony for high achievers, extra coaching for tough subjects and weak students, regular workshops to enhance student’s skill set. The Technical co-curricular and extra-curricular activities of the students covered through FELCOM (Forum of Electronics and Communication) and IEEE include participation in Paper Presentation, Circuit Design, Robotics Contest, Mock Interviews, Seminars, Quiz, Technical competitions, Project Presentation. Also, cultural and sports activities conducted through FELCOM include events such as Sports Day, Teachers Day, Fresher’s Welcome, Farewell to Final Year students and Pooja celebrations. A strong positive reputation of the department draws reputed core companies like NTT Data, Mphasis, Accenture others for campus recruitment. Those candidates desiring to pursue further studies are also doing well in their respective fields. The qualities inculcated in students make them not only good engineers but also good human being.

ADVANCED INFRASTRUCTURE

The state-of-the-art laboratories in the department complement the high standards set by the competitive syllabus and nurture the inclination of the students towards Research and Development, besides giving them the necessary and sufficient backing of practical knowledge that they need for industries. The department has CISCO CCNA , ARM University Labs for students , so that students get a hands-on knowledge about Industry certification courses

DEPARTMENT VISION

To impart quality technical education in Electronics and Communication Engineering with professional skills and human values to address the evolving societal needs.

DEPARTMENT MISSION

  • M1: To provide Technical Education through Effective Teaching Learning Process.
  • M2: To empower students through Research, Innovation and Industry Readiness Programs.
  • M3: To nurture students with positive attitude and ethical values to serve the society.

PROGRAMME SPECIFIC OUTCOMES(PSO)

  • PSO1: Apply the conceptual knowledge of Electronics and Communication Engineering in the analysis and design of Analog - Digital Circuits and Systems and multidisciplinary environment.
  • PSO2: Demonstrate technical competency and apply the domain knowledge in the implementation and performance analysis in the field of Signal Processing, Internet Of Things, Communication, Embedded system and Networking

PROGRAMME EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES(PEO)

  • PEO1: To Prepare competent graduates with solid foundation in the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering.
  • PEO2: To impart analytical and design skills to solve real life engineering problems for the betterment of society.
  • PEO3: Graduates will exhibit ethics, leadership qualities and engage in life long learning to have a successful career.

CURRICULUM

The VTU prescribed Curriculum is designed to prepare students for graduate training in some specialized area of electronics and communication, to prepare students for jobs in industry, business or government, and to provide support courses for students in engineering, mathematics and other fields requiring electronics knowledge.

Placements at Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering

The career opportunities for Electronics & Communication Engineering graduates are plenty and growing. Programming and software development, information systems operation and management, telecommunications and networking, computer science research, web and Internet, graphics and multimedia, training and support, and computer industry specialists are some of the opportunities the graduates find.

The Department in association with Placement Department is conducting in-house placement training from 4th to 6th Semester equipping them to crack the technical aspects of the recruitment. Value addition such as live industry associated projects starting during 6th semester, the Department having MOUs with prestigious Companies, make them more confident to attend the interviews

Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering offers Industry Institute Integrated Programs (IILP) to Students to bridge gap between Industry and Academia and make them industry ready by 6th semester. The students are trained for certification by certified faculty coordinators. The students have cleared the company certification exams and are placed in Multinational Companies.
