ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

With its first batch graduating in 2013, Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at East Point College of Engineering and Technology has evolved with time and offers bachelor degree course. The department vision and mission is to meet the demands for new inventions and technologies by producing knowledgeable and committed technologists to serve our country and humanity as a whole. Forum activities (ELFORIA) conducted by the department has been helpful in developing the individuality of the students. Department has been regularly organizing workshops, technical seminars, paper presentations for the betterment of students and making them industry ready. Alumni meet is conducted every year and we have received continuous support from them for the growth of the department.

DYNAMIC FACULTY

The Department faculty is a mix of industrial and academic expertise with highly qualified and experienced Professors, and Assistant Professors.A majority of the faculty have Ph.D from elite universities and institutions from India and many come with a strong R&D background. The quality of teaching and training has yielded high growth rate of placement at various Companies. The major areas of faculty expertise of the department include Digital Electronics and Communication Systems, Optical Communication and Networks, Signal Processing, Image Processing, Power Electronics, Microwaves, VLSI and Embedded Systems etc.

INDUSTRY INTEGRATED LEARNING PROGRAM(IILP)

Industry Integrated Learning Program (IILP) is an initiative at East Point to bridge the gap between Academia & Industry. The department is associated with companies like CISCO CCNA, ARM University, etc. Dedicated faculties are assigned as educators from the companies are trained, certified and are authorized to train the students on the courses. IILP courses are offered to students right from the 2 nd year of Engineering, so that by the end of engineering, students are enriched with wide variety of technical skills. Centre of Excellence lab for CISCO, ARM University are set up within the campus.

VIBRANT LEARNING EXPERIENCES

EEE Department is actively involved with the student’s growth through regular Parents-Teacher interaction, Distinction Award Ceremony for high achievers, extra coaching for tough subjects and weak students, regular workshops to enhance student’s skill set. The Technical co-curricular and extra-curricular activities of the students covered through Forum activities (ELFORIA) conducted by the department has been helpful in developing the individuality of the students and IEEE include participation in Paper Presentation, Circuit Design, Robotics Contest, Mock Interviews, Seminars, Quiz, Technical competitions, Project Presentation. Also, cultural and sports activities conducted through FELCOM include events such as Sports Day, Teachers Day, Fresher’s Welcome, Farewell to Final Year students and Pooja celebrations. A strong positive reputation of the department draws reputed core companies like NTT Data, Mphasis, Accenture others for campus recruitment

ADVANCED INFRASTRUCTURE

The state-of-the-art laboratories in the department complement the high standards set by the competitive syllabus and nurture the inclination of the students towards Research and Development, besides giving them the necessary and sufficient backing of practical knowledge that they need for industries. The department has CISCO CCNA , ARM University Labs for students , so that students get a hands-on knowledge about Industry certification courses

DEPARTMENT VISION

VISION: To impart quality education in the field of Electrical & Electronics Engineering and to produce globally competent engineers to serve the society.

DEPARTMENT MISSION

  • M1: To educate the student to become better practicing engineers to meet global excellence.
  • M2: To provide better environment through latest developments in electrical engineering involving problem solving, design, practice and training.
  • M3: To motivate the graduates to become a good leader, designer and researcher through industry-oriented trainings with social and ethical responsibilities.

PROGRAMME SPECIFIC OUTCOMES(PSO)

  • PSO1: To train the students to meet the global technical challenges.
  • PSO2: To prepare the students to be morally upright and contributing to nation building and progress of humanity.
  • PSO3: To create applied research-oriented mind-set and focus in fulfilling growing demands of society through mentoring and motivation.
  • PSO4: To accelerate the engineering innovations for transition to sustainable energy.

PROGRAMME EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES(PEO)

  • PEO1: To train the students of Electrical Engineering program so that they can work with government or private sector companies responsible for development of power sector & prove themselves in electrical maintenance for the industry.
  • PEO2: To train students of Electrical Engineering program who can contribute to teaching profession, research & development by pursuing higher studies.
  • PEO3: To train students of Electrical Engineering program in a manner that they should function effectively in the multicultural and multidisciplinary groups in their practice of Electrical Engineering profession.

DEPARTMENT OF ELECTRICAL & ELECTRONICS COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

CURRICULUM

The VTU PRESSCRIBED Curriculum is designed to prepare students for graduate training in some specialized area of electrical and electronics, to prepare students for jobs in industry, business or government, and to provide support courses for students in engineering, mathematics and other fields requiring electrical & electronics knowledge.

Placements at Department of Computer Science and Engineering

The career opportunities for Computer Science and Engineering graduates are plenty and growing. Programming and software development, information systems operation and management, telecommunications and networking, computer science research, web and Internet, graphics and multimedia, training and support, and computer industry specialists are some of the opportunities the graduates find.

The Department in association with Placement Department is conducting in-house placement training from 4th to 6th Semester equipping them to crack the technical aspects of the recruitment. Value addition such as live industry associated projects starting during 6th semester, the Department having MOUs with prestigious Companies, make them more confident to attend the interviews.

Department of Computer Science & Engineering offers Industry Institute Integrated Programs (IILP) to Students to bridge gap between Industry and Academia and make them industry ready by 6 th semester. The students are trained for certification by certified faculty coordinators. The students have cleared the company certification exams and are placed in Multinational Companies.
