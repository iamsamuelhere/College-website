ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

The department of Basic Sciences Engineering and Humanities is a first platform of all the aspiring engineers step on to. It strongly believes that knowledge is the base of all basic sciences and it strives to achieve this power to its best. This Department is devoted to foster the fundamental principles and understanding of Science to enhance the students’ basic knowledge of Engineering. It offers excellent introductory courses in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and English which will both instruct and stimulate students in all of the University's programs

DEPARTMENT OF MATHEMATICS

Mathematics is the queen of all sciences. We can neither know things correctly nor can have practical utility of computation unless we have the knowledge of Engineering Mathematics. Since Mathematics has cultural and disciplinary values. It occupies an important place in the curriculum and in society also.
The Department of Mathematics was established in the year1999. The Department of Mathematics has been recognised to carry out Research by VTU, Belagavi. Currently seven research scholars are pursuing part-time Ph.D program. The thrust areas of Research of the department include Computational Fluid Dynamics & Graph Theory.

DEPARTMENT OF ENGINEERING PHYSICS

The department of physics was established in the year 1999 as a supporting department to all engineering programmes. The department imparts education in basic science both in theory and practical knowledge. The objective is to lay the basic firm foundation required by the engineers to plan for appropriate applications of the matter.
The department is well established with a spacious laboratory added with advanced devices, equipment and instruments to attract and to motivate young minds.

CHEMISTRY DEPARTMENT

Chemistry Department has a well- equipped Engineering Chemistry Laboratory having the major equipments like Potentiometers, Colorimeters, Flame Photometers, pH meters, Conductivity meters, Digital electronic weighing Balance, Electric Hot-air Oven etc. catering to the needs of the B.E. 1st year students according to VTU curriculum.

HUMANITIES DEPARTMENT

The Department of Basic Science and Humanities are responsible for conduct of 1st year classes for all B.E. degree programs offered by core departments. The department coordinates the 1st year classes with various core departments in addition to teaching fundamental Basic Science subjects, Technical English & Constitution of India & Professional Ethics. The students go through two cycles of academic semester, each cycle configured under Physics group and Chemistry group.

DEPARTMENT VISION

VISION: To empower the students with ethical values and fundamental knowledge of Basic Sciences needed for engineering education with aim to transform them into a complete engineer dedicated to the service of society, nation and the humanity.

DEPARTMENT MISSION

  • To motivate and prepare the students to excel in the physical, chemical and mathematical sciences along with other technical subjects through teaching and research to achieve their educational objectives.
  • To inculcate the basic moral and ethical values in students besides the technical education in order to mould them into a responsible citizen.
