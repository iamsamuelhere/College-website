ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

Department of Mechanical Engineering was established in 1999 with UG program with 120 intakes, PG program established in 2010 with Product Design and Manufacturing, Thermal Power Engineering with intake of 18 each. The department envisages strengthening the interactions with the Industry. The department constantly assures that quality technical education is imparted to the students and accomplishes excellence in curricular and co-curricular activities on par with the other prestigious institutes of the country.

DYNAMIC FACULTY

The Department has well qualified and experienced faculties and competent enough to undertake teaching, research and consultancy services. The faculties are specialized in such diverse fields of mechanical engineering like CAD/CAM lab, Material Testing & Metallurgy lab, Fluid Mechanics & Fluid Machinery lab, Thermal Engineering Lab, Foundry lab, Machine Dynamics Lab and Metrology Lab. All the faculty members have highest commitment to achieve excellence in teaching and to have better interaction with the students the faculty members are involved in students counseling. The faculties are competent enough to offer courses and research in many fields of Civil Engineering.

DEPARTMENT VISION

VISION: To be the centre for transforming the students as professional mechanical engineers through imparting, analytical and problem solving skills to solve the societal problems and serve the humanity.

DEPARTMENT MISSION

MISSION:
  • M1: Empower students in higher and applied areas of mechanical engineering.
  • M2: Collaborate with industries, research organization for internship and joint research in order to enhance innovative and managerial practices.
  • M3: To inculcate entrepreneurial skill and human values to meet needs of the society.

PROGRAMME SPECIFIC OUTCOMES(PSO)

  • PSO1: Graduate shall be employable in the field of engineering to analyse, interpret and provide solutions to engineering and social problems.
  • PSO2: Adapt to dynamic challenges in industries of society and hold positions of high responsibility.

PROGRAMME EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES(PEO)

  • PEO-1: Graduates shall have successful careers as Mechanical Engineers, to lead and manage teams.
  • PEO-2: Graduates shall be professional in engineering practice and socially responsible.
  • PEO-3: Graduates shall be pursuing advanced education research and engage in the process of lifelong learning.
  • PEO-4: Graduates shall have technical & computing skill learning attitude to create novel products & solutions from outreach industry ease technology.

Placements at Department of Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineers play a vital role in varied industries such as automotive, manufacturing, automation, aerospace, biotechnology, computers and electronics, chemical, construction, energy conversion and more. A Mechanical Engineer can work in both private or public companies. The degree holders are acquired by many big firms both in India and abroad.

Several fortune companies like Infosys, TCs, WIPRO and many more visit our campus for campus recruitments and end up with good amount of students getting placed.

Department of Mechanical Engineering offers Industry Institute Integrated Programs (IILP) to Students to bridge gap between Industry and Academia and make them industry ready by 6 th semester. The students are trained for certification by certified faculty coordinators. The students have cleared the company certification exams and are placed in Multinational Companies.
