ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

The Department of Information Science and Engineering was established in the year 2001 with an intake of 120 students, which is affiliated to VTU, Belagavi. The guiding principle of ISE is to develop the best IT infrastructure, a world-class learning and research environment, and to promote moral and ethical principles through industry cooperation. Our department embraces the Flipped classroom approach which is the Online learning Platform, allowing our Young aspirants to think beyond the horizon. Our sheer size and scale mean we can offer unparalleled opportunities for interdisciplinary ventures and collaborative working, enabling both academics and students to achieve great things here.

DYNAMIC FACULTY

The strength of the ISE department is the cohesive team of faculty members who work in grooming the “Creators of Innovative Technology” and “Outstanding Computer Professionals with ethical and human values”. Our faculties are highly educated and well trained in aspects of industry and research, and they endeavour to provide the greatest quality classroom instruction to achieve the College's and Department's Vision/Mission

INDUSTRY INTEGRATED LEARNING PROGRAM(IILP)

The Departments works closely with Reputed IT firms that help in bridging the gap between the Industry and Academics through various Academic-Alliance Programmes like AWS, Salesforce, UiPath RPA, VMware, Google Cloud, Cisco. Our faculties are certified under these programmes and they are authorized as educators to train students. Students also undergo training programmes conducted by INFOSYS and ORACLE. Dedicated labs for RPA, Salesforce, AWS are available for students to get hands-on experience on these technologies. IILP has opened doors of opportunities for our students to get placed in these reputed organizations

VIBRANT LEARNING EXPERIENCES

Our department offers various clubs and activities, giving them the opportunity to explore their interests outside of the classroom. Students are encouraged to experience the real working environment through internships at reputable IT firms. The Department conducts training in Technical, Aptitude, and Soft Skills for all students with a view to equipping them with the skills required to conquer the rigorous Interview process. The growing skills and competence of our students have paved way for a good number of final year students to get placed in reputed IT Organizations.

ADVANCED INFRASTRUCTURE

The department has an excellent infrastructure and computing equipments supported by various laboratories and high-speed Internet facilities. Our department is honeycombed with well-equipped classrooms that supports multimedia-based education and well stocked library with the latest books, Journals and research publications, making it a perfect location to maximize one's potential

DEPARTMENT VISION

To be a renowned centre promoting the students to technically proficient in the field of information science and engineering by engaging them the knowledge on emerging technologies and ethics for the betterment of society.

DEPARTMENT MISSION

  • To transform students to achieve academic excellence as well as professional competence to meet the growing industrial needs.
  • To nurture life-long learning among students in information science and engineering for their continuous growth in IT industry.
  • To strengthen links with industries through partnership and collaborative developmental works for upgrading knowledge sharing among students and faculties

PROGRAMME SPECIFIC OUTCOMES(PSO)

  • PSO 1: Ability to design, develop, implement computer programs using software engineering standards and strategies to facilitate different practices of IT Industry
  • POS 2: Understand the process that support the delivery and management of information systems with a specific application environment.

PROGRAMME EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES(PEO)

  • PEO1: To become qualified and competent information technology professional equipped with continual learning skills.
  • PE02: Enhance the skills to provide innovative solutions to real world problems by using modern tools and technologies.
  • PEO3: Graduates of this program will emerge as efficient professionals having abilities to maintain and enhance professional skills required to fulfil the needs of society in solving technical problems using engineering principles, tools and practices in an ethical and responsible manner.

CURRICULUM

The VTU prescribed Curriculum is designed to prepare students for graduate training in some specialized area of information science, to prepare students for jobs in industry, business or government, and to provide support courses for students in engineering, mathematics and other fields requiring information science knowledge.

Placements at Department of Information Science and Engineering

The Department of Information Science and Engineering have a Placement team dedicated to assisting and supporting current undergraduate students in each step of getting Jobs, searching and applying for internships, and to ensure each student meets the industrial experience requirements. AWS, Salesforce, VMware, CISCO, UiPath, Oracle, Google Cloud are some of the Industry Integrated Programmes offered by the Department, which bridge the gap between industry and academia. The students undergo rigorous corporate readiness programmes like Aptitude training, soft skills training to outshine the Recruitment process. The growing skills and competence of our students have paved way for a good number of final year students to get placed in reputed IT organizations.
