HOSTEL FACILITY


East Point provides boarding for both boys and girls with furnished single occupied, twin sharing and three sharing accommodation. Hostel Number of Students in Boys Hostel is 339 Nos. Number of Students in Girls Hostel is 200 Nos. At EPCET, we provide clean surrounds so that students can live in an amiable atmosphere. Discipline is given utmost importance to prevent any untoward incidents like ragging and other offences. EPCET genuinely ensures a homely feeling to the through its ambience.

Hostel Facilities:

  • Common reading room
  • Spacious Dining hall
  • R.O. purified drinking water
  • 24X7 Medical assistance with Ambulance
