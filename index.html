About East Point College of Engineering & Technology


Welcome to the East Point College of Engineering and Technology. The East Point College of Engineering and Technology (EPCET) was established under the aegis of M G Charitable Trust founded by the Educational Visionary Dr. S M Venkatpathi, in the year 1999. Barron land of around 90 acres was transformed into a lush green admirable academic campus within a period of two years. State of the art structures, roads, Electricity connections, water and all other amenities were created afresh. Presently over 7000 students are pursuing their Technical education in various streams of BE, M. Tech. and PhD. East Point College of Engineering and Technology at Bidarahalli, Virgo Nagar, Bangalore is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi and approved by AICTE, Delhi. One of the best campus for placement activities has a record placement in all the reputed national and multinational companies.

At East Point, we vow to bring Humanity back into Education. We aim to accomplish our goal by practicing collective learning, sustainability in community living and by encouraging people-friendly environments on our campus. We pledge to live by our philosophy in everything that we do. For building a Better Mankind and a Better World

Our students graduate with the ability to adapt to an intellectually and technologically changing environment. Over the years, we have accomplished this with the participative efforts of the management, staff, students and parents.

Education in EPCET is highly committed and responsive to the current trends and needs in the global market and industry. To bridge the gap between industry & Academia, the college offers Industry Institute Integrated learning programs as value added course to all branches of engineering students like UiPath RPA, AWS, Salesforce, Google Cloud Ready Program, Oracle Academy certifications, CISCO CCNA , ARM University etc. Many students are certified and placed in Multinational companies.
