Our Campus Life

The lush green campus offers delightful spaces for studying, living and interaction. the excellent residential facilities have been carefully planned according to international standards. our hostels on the campus offer well-ventilated, comfortable rooms stringent, round-the-clock security arrangements and medical facility from our own East Point hospital, which is located within the same campus, common recreational areas help develop a sense of togetherness and foster long-term bonds. the college cafeteria, equipped to serve hundreds of students.


Cultural Activities

At EPCET, students pursue their interests through diverse communities: from debates to the various clubs and teams reflect the wide range of personal and professional interests of our vibrant, student community. Every year to keep the cultural spirit alive within the students, the Institution conducts a college fest called Samskruthi. Students and faculties are encouraged to take part in various cultural events which helps students to develop a sense of responsibility and strengthen their confidence.


Institute Social Responsibility

Social Responsibilities include tree plantation, environmental awareness, Blood Donation Camps, educating rural kids on computer literacy, voting awareness etc.

Sports

From cricket to basketball, athletics to yoga – students can stay fit in any way they choose. Excellent coaching facilities allow them to sharpen their skills and EPCET teams have won acclaim for their performance on the sports field.
